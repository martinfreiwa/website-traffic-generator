A website traffic generator works by sending traffic to a website.

- **Sending bots to visit the site**
- **Generating traffic through a network of sites**
- **Using software to simulate traffic to the site**

Once the traffic has been generated, it is directed to the
              website. This traffic can help to improve the site's search engine
              rankings and visibility, as search engines consider traffic to be
              an indicator of a site's popularity and relevance.
