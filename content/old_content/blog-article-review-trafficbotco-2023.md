## [#25 Trafficbot.co**](https://trafficbot.co/)

![Somiibo landing page screenshot.](assets/img/blog/traffic-bots-reviews/traffic-bot-co.webp)

## Introduction

If you want to boost website traffic, TrafficBot.co
                        might be the solution you need. In this review, we will
                        look closer at the features, pricing, support, and
                        traffic quality offered by this platform, so you can
                        make an informed decision.

## Dashboard

The user dashboard of TrafficBot.co is well-organized
                        and easy to navigate. It shows your projects and options
                        to purchase traffic plans, making managing your active
                        schedules and subscriptions simple. With its
                        straightforward interface, you can easily monitor your
                        website traffic and adjust settings to maximize results.

![Somiibo dashboard screenshot.](assets/img/blog/traffic-bots-reviews/trafficbotco-dashboard.webp)

## Pricing

TrafficBot.co offers competitive pricing with three
                        different plans, starting from just $9/month for 65k
                        guaranteed pageviews. The platform provides essential
                        features such as duration control, traffic scheduling,
                        referrer source customization, and more. As you upgrade
                        to higher plans, you'll enjoy additional benefits like
                        increased guaranteed page views and longer maximum
                        session duration. Overall, the pricing is affordable and
                        offers good value for money.

![Trafficbot.co pricing screenshot.](assets/img/blog/traffic-bots-reviews/trafficbotco-pricing.webp)

## Support

Although TrafficBot.co only provides support through a
                        broad FAQ section and email, they promise a quick
                        response time of 24 to 48 hours. However, there may be
                        better options for users who require immediate
                        assistance or have urgent issues. The lack of live chat
                        or phone support could be a drawback for some users.

![Trafficbot.co support chat screenshot.](assets/img/blog/traffic-bots-reviews/trafficbotco-support.webp)

## Traffic Quality

TrafficBot.co has received positive reviews for their
                        traffic quality, which suggests they deliver
                        high-quality visitors to your website. The platform
                        offers several features to customize and improve the
                        quality of your traffic, such as geo-targeting, bounce
                        rate control, and visitor diversification. Google
                        Analytics is also safe and compatible with AdSense and
                        other pay-per-click affiliate programs.
### Refund Policy

It is important to note that TrafficBot.co does not
                        offer refunds. They provide free traffic for users to
                        try their service before purchasing, which helps to
                        prevent abuse of the refund policy. Suppose you
                        encounter any issues with traffic not showing up on
                        Google Analytics. In that case, you can contact their
                        support team for assistance.
## Conclusion

TrafficBot.co is a user-friendly and affordable solution
                        for website owners looking to increase their traffic.
                        It's competitive pricing and comprehensive features
                        offer good value for money. While the lack of immediate
                        support options may concern some users, the platform's
                        overall quality and performance make it a viable option
                        for boosting website traffic. If you're searching for a
                        cost-effective way to improve your website's visibility,
