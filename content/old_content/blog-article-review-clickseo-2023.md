## What Are SERP's?

SERP stands for Search Engine Results Page. It refers to the page that appears when you enter a search query on a search engine like**Google, Bing, or Yahoo**. The page displays a list of results relevant to the query entered. Each result typically includes a title, a short description, and a URL link to the webpage that contains the information.The SERP also includes various features such as sponsored results, featured snippets, knowledge graphs, and images, depending on the search query and the search engine used. The aim of a SERP is to provide users with the most relevant and useful information related to their search query.
## What is SERP Traffic?

SERP, or Search Engine Results Page, traffic refers to the visitors your website attracts through search engine results pages. When someone enters a query into a search engine like Google, the engine displays a list of relevant results on its SERP.
